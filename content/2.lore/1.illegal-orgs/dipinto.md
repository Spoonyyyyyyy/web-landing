# The backstory behind the Di Pinto's

After major success on distant islands in the form of robbery, murder and distribution of weapons and narcotics, the ***Di'Pintos*** set their gaze upon the city of Los Santos searching for the fortune they dream of after the name ***Di'Pinto*** became too known to law enforcement agencies overseas to continue their crime spree without risking the demise of their brotherhood.

The leader Kay Di'Pinto, known as "Kay Dee" has molded his members to be ruthless killers, ambitious thinkers and incredibly dangerous individuals. After uniting with his other high command upon arriving to Los Santos, Kay Dee started to make contact with his former gang members to re-establish the infamous criminal syndicate and make the ***Di'Pintos*** known again.

Wasting no time and making no small name for themselves, the maverick gang of outlaws began work in the city of Los Santos. The gang members known as “soldiers” are known to fear no man, with some members being former special forces, and others being criminal rejects of society, when together, they are a band of brothers, willing to do whatever it takes to further their agenda.

Although they are feared, they are also very prosperous weapons and narcotic distributors and are known to be very honorable dealers. They tend to treat people with the same regard that they are given but coming from the streets, are intolerant to the disdainful. Curiously there have been multiple reports of the Di'Pintos spreading their wealth to the less fortunate.

The gang seeks to reach new heights in the city of Los Santos, while also maintaining its good reputation, whether that be through making alliances to deal with, protecting those who can’t protect themselves, robbing the rich and powerful, and pushing the limits of their skills to unseen extremes. There is a saying amongst the **Di'Pinto** Soldiers, “Nitimur in Vetitum.” meaning “We strive for the forbidden”.
